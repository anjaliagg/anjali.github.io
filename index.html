Pursuits

Although Time is a relative concept, this is how I spend it.

Travel Blogging

Travel fills the life in me. Talking to people from different walks of life and enjoying the silences of mother nature constitutes travel for me. I document & share my solo travel stories while at it.

Photography

This for me is a way to present the world as I observe and experience it. Sometimes it is candid, sometimes a well thought and dreamt of idea be it Nature, Street or Wedding photography.

Creative Writing

I find myself writing when I need to empty my vessel of thoughts. It instantly makes me feel better. Life and Human behaviour intrigue me, thus I pour my thoughts in the form of poetry or prose.

Art by Me

Welcome to an online Exhibition of my poems, photographs and artwork. Enjoy!
